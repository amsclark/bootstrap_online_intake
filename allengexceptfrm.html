This online application allows you to apply for services from Legal Aid of Nebraska. It is available 24/7. You may need 20-30 minutes to complete the application. Information you provide on the application will be held in confidence.

Do not use this application if you have an emergency, such as a deadline or hearing within 14 days.
The application process:

  1. Complete and submit the application.
  2. After you submit your application, you should receive immediate confirmation that your application was submitted.
  3. You should receive another email from us within 5 business days to let you know your application has been processed.
  4. If it looks like you qualify for services from Legal Aid of Nebraska, we will schedule a call to you to talk to you about your legal issue. The call will be scheduled based upon the best times you provide us in the application. We will tell you by email what date and time we will call you.
  5. If it looks like you do not qualify for services from Legal Aid of Nebraska, we will tell you by email. We will NOT schedule a call to you.
