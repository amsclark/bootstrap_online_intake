Do not use this application if you have an emergency, such as a deadline or hearing within 14 days.

Instead, please call us at 402-348-1060, or toll-free at 877-250-2016, Monday and Wednesday, 9 am — 11 am CST, and on Tuesday and Thursday from 1:30 pm — 3:30 pm CST.

You must have an email address to use this online application.

We will reply to you by email.

We will ask you questions about your monthly household income and assets. If you do not have that information for all members of your household, please gather it, and then complete the application.

You must be 60 years of age or older, or be assisting someone 60 years of age or older with their legal issue.

Legal Aid of Nebraska will use the information you provide to see if you qualify for our services. However, completing an application does NOT mean that Legal Aid of Nebraska will accept your case. Legal Aid of Nebraska cannot assist everyone who applies for help. Completing an application does NOT create an attorney-client relationship. And, it does NOT mean you are a client of Legal Aid of Nebraska.

Legal Aid of Nebraska cannot help with the following

  • • Traffic tickets
  • • Criminal cases (unless they are filed in tribal court)
  • • Worker's compensation cases
  • • Personal injury cases

It is ok to have someone help you complete this application. The application should be completed using your personal information. We have to talk to you unless you have someone who is authorized to speak on your behalf.

If you are Power of Attorney for the applicant, we can talk to you directly if you provide the Power of Attorney Document. Please complete the application in the applicant's name, indicate on the application that you are the power of attorney and send us the Power of Attorney document by email at accesslineattorneys@legalaidofnebraska.com, or by fax to (402) 348-1065, Attention: ElderAccessLine®, or by mail at ElderAccessLine® 1904 Farnam St., Suite 500, Omaha, NE 68102.

You should expect to receive an email response about your application from Legal Aid of Nebraska within 5 business days. If it looks like you qualify for services from Legal Aid, we will schedule a call for you to talk to an intake paralegal. We will schedule the call based upon the best times you provide us in the application. We will tell you what day and time we will call you in our email response to you. If you do not hear back from us, it is your responsibility to contact us at the number above. Legal Aid of Nebraska is not responsible for any technical or human errors that may occur when processing your application.
