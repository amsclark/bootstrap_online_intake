This online form allows you to submit your Beginning Farmer and/or Transitioning question to Legal Aid of Nebraska. It is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. You may need 10 to 20 minutes to complete the form. Information you provide on the form will be held in confidence.

Do not use this form if you have an emergency, such as a deadline or hearing within 14 days. Instead, please call 855-660-1391 or 800-464-0258 from 8:00 a.m. — 4:30 p.m. CST. The Application Process:
  1. Complete and submit the form.
  2. After you submit your application, you should receive immediate confirmation that your application was submitted.
  3. You should receive another email from us within 5 business days responding to your question.

Legal Aid of Nebraska, with funding from the United States Department of Agriculture's National Institute of Food and Agriculture, provides this online form to assist farmers or ranchers:

  1. with ten years or less of farming or ranching experience, or
  2. who are near-retirement or retired and are considering business transition and/or estate planning.

IF YOU HAVE QUESTIONS UNRELATED TO YOUR FARMING OR RANCHING OPERATION, YOU MAY STILL QUALIFY FOR OTHER SERVICES PROVIDED BY LEGAL AID OF NEBRASKA.

If your question is unrelated to your farming or ranching operation, please contact Legal Aid of Nebraska's AccessLine® at 402-348-1060 or 800-250-2016 on Monday and Wednesday 9:00 am — 11:00 am CST and on Tuesday and Thursday 1:30 pm — 3:30 pm CST. You may also apply online at www.legalaidofnebraska.org

Legal Aid of Nebraska cannot help with the following
  • • Traffic tickets
  • • Criminal cases (unless they are filed in tribal court)
  • • Worker's compensation cases
  • • Personal injury cases
